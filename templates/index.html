Our Courses Workflow

Identifying the course

Begin by clearly defining the task or concept that you want to teach. This could be anything from learning a new programming language to understanding a scientific concept.

Breaking the task into Parts

Break down the task into its parts or sub-skills. For example, if the task is to learn how to program in Java , sub-skills might include basic syntax, data types, etc.

Learning Modules

Engage with course materials, including video lectures, readings, assignments, and quizzes. Our modular approach allows you to progress through the course at your own pace,

Flexibility

Allow learners to navigate freely between different parts of the task. Provide options for learners to revisit previous sections or skip ahead if they feel confident in their understanding.

Assessments

Receive feedback and assessment on your progress through quizzes, assignments, and assessments. Our instructors provide constructive feedback to help you improve and succeed in the course.

Interactive Activities

Break down learning tasks into interactive activities such as quizzes, exercises, or simulations. These activities can reinforce learning objectives and provide opportunities for hands-on practice.
